    About Hear Clear

    Hear Clear is a system that use to analyse any sound or music given by user.

    How to use us :

    You need to upload the audio into Hear Clear. Once the audio is saved, the audio will be listed in the list. You need to click on the song title, then Hear Clear will display the waveform picture, file size, sampling frequency, file type and duration of the audio.

    Kindly ask us for any help !
